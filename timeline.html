Modernization: Greek Mythology in Film & TV

Influential adaptations of Greek mythology in cinema and television

Pick your media!

1997: Hercules (Disney)

An animated feature that brings the myth of Heracles to a new generation, blending ancient stories with modern humor and music.

2004: Troy

A big-budget film depicting the Trojan War, starring Brad Pitt as Achilles and featuring gods and heroes from Greek mythology.

2010: Clash of the Titans

A fantasy film following Perseus as he battles mythical creatures and the gods of Olympus.

2010: Percy Jackson

A fantasy film of an adaptation on one of the most popular YA novels

2017: Wonder Woman

A superhero film that draws on Greek mythology, featuring Diana as an Amazonian demigoddess and referencing gods like Ares and Zeus.

2024: Kaos (TV Series)

A modern retelling of Greek myths, focusing on the gods and their influence in a contemporary setting.

2026: The Odyssey

The first modern retelling of The Odyssey with an ensemble cast and a talented director.
